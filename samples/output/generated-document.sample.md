# Vendor Approval SOP

## Purpose

Define a consistent process for reviewing and approving new vendors before they are added to the vendor master.

## Scope

This sample applies to a generic vendor approval workflow using synthetic public data.

## Process

1. A requester submits a vendor intake form with business justification and contact details.
2. Procurement reviews the vendor for category fit, risk indicators, and required documentation.
3. Finance validates payment details and confirms that required tax information is complete.
4. Legal reviews contract terms when a contract or statement of work is required.
5. The approving owner records the final decision and approved vendors are added to the vendor master.

## Controls

- Required fields must be complete before review begins.
- Payment details should be validated before vendor activation.
- Contract review is required when legal terms are attached.
- Approved vendors should be reviewed annually.

## Assumptions And Limits

- This sample uses mock generation and local retrieval context.
- No external provider, production data, private document, or real vendor record is required.
